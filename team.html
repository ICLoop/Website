TEAM LEADS

Designing a Hyperloop pod requires all the different aspects of the pod to be split into smaller subgroups. The leads are responsible for organising their smaller teams to successfully deliver their aspect of the pod. Our team of leads get to put into use their project management skills as well as developing basic managerial and organizational skills which they take into their career in the future.

AERODYNAMICS

We are in charge of designing and manufacturing the aerodynamic shell, which encloses all of the subsystems on the pod. This gives us a significant reduction in drag, allowing us to reach speeds of up to 400 km/h!

PROPULSION

We are developing a custom double-sided linear induction motor that will generate enough thrust to meet top speeds of up to 400 km/h. The motor will deliver this high performance without overheating or compromising the structural integrity of the chassis.

BUSINESS TEAM

The Business team is in charge of all the work that is not engineering and pod development related. We split the Business team into the Communication and the Outreach team to distribute the various duties invovled to ensure the project is a success. The Communication team takes charge of managing the outward image of the project such as social media, website, and others whilst trying to advertise and push out the message of this project. The Outreach team focuses more on reaching out to potential sponsors and industrial partners to secure funding for the project to move forward and to develop industrial connections to help with the project.
