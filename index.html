Our mission

Revolutionise Hyperloop technology
Our goal is to produce one of the world’s first fully scalable pods. Through the use of custom Linear Induction Motors, rotating Hallbach arrays and advanced composites, we aim to hit speeds in excess of 400 km/h with a 2m-long pod. Imperial has been a pioneer in the field of motor technology: the first in the world to build a full-scale linear induction motor. Our team will continue this legacy.

Redefine human transport
Hyperloop will unlock the next level of transport. Our technology will forge a new, efficient and fast way to travel: Hyperloop will shape the scope of urban development, unleash untapped economic potential and make long commutes a distant memory.

Reclaim our future
Technology is the key to a green future. If we want to tackle climate change, reduce urban congestion and take the first step to life on another planet – we need a new approach. ICLoop is leading the charge: providing solutions today to solve tomorrows’ problems.
